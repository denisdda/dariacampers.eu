Katamarano 50 - 6 seats

You want to travel with a lot of extra space and comfort?

Then look no further, rent this caravan with their unique features: This mobile home is equipped with 8 sleeping places that are used during parking and 6 places with seat belts (including the driver's and passenger's seats in the car cabin) to be used while driving.

It has a double bed above the cabin, a central sofa that turns into a double bed and 4 single beds in the back (2 bunk beds on each side) above the luggage compartment. The front door of the residential module and 4 ventilated windows equipped with mosquito nets.

There is a bathroom with toilet, shower and sink. The kitchen is equipped with a stove with 3 burners and a sink, as well as a refrigerator with freezer running at 12 / 220V and gas. The motorhome is equipped with a tank for clean water - 100L. Dirty water tank - 130L. Bio toilet tank - 15L. Responsible for heating is the Truma Combi 4 system with integrated hot water boiler. Electric lighting and output sockets - 12 V. Electrical wiring and 220V sockets for use in equipped camping.

The chassis of the vehicle is equipped with a 130 hp engine and a 6 + 1 manual gearbox, diesel consumption per 100 km - 9.6 liters, electric windows, electric mirrors, assistance at the beginning of the movement, ABS and ESP systems, air conditioned.
