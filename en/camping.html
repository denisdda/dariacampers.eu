Camping in Romania

The weather is changing, but the holiday remains the holiday!


RO RU

This year things have taken an unfavorable turn, due to the appearance of a new virus, a new strain, which was not identified until recently in humans. The epidemic caused by this new virus is still ongoing, and social distancing measures must be promoted!


We consider that at this moment, venturing on a caravan trip will not make you feel exposed, but on the contrary, you will feel relaxed, protected; you can choose your own camping place, you can delimit your comfort zone! The main advantage would be that you can use the caravan as a means of transport and as accommodation! You will not feel under any circumstances the lack of hotel conditions, and the conditions for sleeping and spending free time are among the highest! In addition, what more can we say about the challenge of some wonderful mornings spent in the mountains, in the fresh air, smelling strongly of fir and grass!



We get asked quite often, "Am I allowed to take my pet with me?"


We strongly believe that pets play a very important role in the lives of many of us. The Answer is: "YES!", but we ask you to let us know in advance! It was definitely a pretty tough time for these little family members too! Below is a happy example, where a beautiful love story unfolds: she from Bucharest, he from Brasov !!!
